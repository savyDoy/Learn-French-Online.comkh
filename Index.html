Lesson 2: Introducing Yourself

Practice introducing yourself in French:

  • Je m'appelle [Your Name] - My name is [Your Name]
  • Enchanté(e) - Nice to meet you
  • Comment ça va? - How are you?
  • Ça va bien, merci. - I'm good, thank you.

Lesson 3: Verbs

Verbs in French are categorized into three groups based on their endings. Here is how to conjugate them:

  • First Group (-er):

    • Example: Parler (to speak)

    • Je parle - I speak
    • Tu parles - You speak
    • Il/Elle parle - He/She speaks
    • Nous parlons - We speak
    • Vous parlez - You (formal/plural) speak
    • Ils/Elles parlent - They speak
  • Second Group (-ir):

    • Example: Finir (to finish)

    • Je finis - I finish
    • Tu finis - You finish
    • Il/Elle finit - He/She finishes
    • Nous finissons - We finish
    • Vous finissez - You (formal/plural) finish
    • Ils/Elles finissent - They finish
  • Third Group (irregular):

    • Example: Être (to be)

    • Je suis - I am
    • Tu es - You are
    • Il/Elle est - He/She is
    • Nous sommes - We are
    • Vous êtes - You (formal/plural) are
    • Ils/Elles sont - They are

    Example: Avoir (to have)

    • J'ai - I have
    • Tu as - You have
    • Il/Elle a - He/She has
    • Nous avons - We have
    • Vous avez - You (formal/plural) have
    • Ils/Elles ont - They have
